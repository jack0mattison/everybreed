Best Low Maintenance Dog Breeds (2026)

Low maintenance dogs need less grooming, less intense exercise or both—though no dog is zero maintenance. The best low-maintenance breeds for the UK are typically short-coated, moderate in energy and easy to keep clean. They still need training, company and daily care.

The breeds below are often cited as lower maintenance in terms of coat care and sometimes exercise. Match the breed to your lifestyle; "low maintenance" doesn't mean no commitment.

1. Labrador Retriever

Short coat; no clipping. But they shed heavily and need lots of exercise.

Size: Medium–Large · Exercise: High · Grooming: Moderate

2. Whippet

Short coat, minimal grooming. Calm indoors; needs a good run.

Size: Medium · Exercise: Moderate–High · Grooming: Low

3. French Bulldog

Short coat, low exercise. Be aware of health and heat sensitivity.

Size: Small · Exercise: Low–Moderate · Grooming: Low

4. Pug

Easy coat care and low exercise. Watch weight and breathing.

Size: Small · Exercise: Low–Moderate · Grooming: Low

5. Staffordshire Bull Terrier

Short coat, minimal grooming. Needs daily exercise and training.

Size: Medium · Exercise: High · Grooming: Low

6. Beagle

Short coat, easy to groom. Needs plenty of exercise and mental stimulation.

Size: Small–Medium · Exercise: High · Grooming: Low

7. Miniature Dachshund

Short coat in smooth variety. Moderate exercise; protect the back.

Size: Small · Exercise: Moderate · Grooming: Low

8. Border Terrier

Wire coat that needs occasional stripping. Hardy and relatively low fuss.

Size: Small · Exercise: Moderate–High · Grooming: Low

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the easiest dog to look after?

Whippets, French Bulldogs and Pugs are often cited as easier in terms of grooming. Labradors are easy to groom but need lots of exercise. Match the dog to your time and energy.
