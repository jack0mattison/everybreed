Frequently Asked Questions

Which is better for a flat, Mini Dachshund or Jack Russell?

Miniature Dachshunds often adapt better to flats with moderate exercise. Jack Russells are very energetic and need more outlet; they can be noisy if under-exercised.

Which has more health issues?

Dachshunds are prone to back (IVDD) problems; Jack Russells to patellar and eye issues. Both benefit from responsible breeding.
