Frequently Asked Questions

Which is better for families, Staffy or American Bulldog?

Staffordshire Bull Terriers are often more compact and have a long reputation as "nanny dogs" in the UK. American Bulldogs are larger and need experienced handling. Both need socialisation.

Which is more common in the UK?

Staffordshire Bull Terriers are far more common in the UK and are a Kennel Club recognised breed. American Bulldogs are less common here.
