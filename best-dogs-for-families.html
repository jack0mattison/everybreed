Best Family Dogs in the UK (2026)

Choosing a family dog in the UK means finding a breed that is patient with children, adaptable to home life and manageable in size and energy. The best family dogs are typically friendly, tolerant and eager to be part of the action without being overly fragile or demanding.

Below we list breeds that consistently rank well for families: good with kids, trainable and suited to British homes and weather. Always supervise young children with any dog and teach kids how to interact safely with pets.

1. Labrador Retriever

Outgoing, loyal and great with children. Needs plenty of exercise and sheds a lot, but worth it for many families.

Size: Medium–Large · Exercise: High · Grooming: Moderate

2. Golden Retriever

Gentle, patient and highly trainable. A classic family favourite that thrives on company and activity.

Size: Large · Exercise: High · Grooming: Moderate

3. Cavalier King Charles Spaniel

Affectionate, small enough for most homes and generally good with children when socialised.

Size: Small · Exercise: Moderate · Grooming: Moderate

4. Cockapoo

Friendly, low-shedding and adaptable. Popular with families who want a smaller, sociable dog.

Size: Small–Medium · Exercise: Moderate · Grooming: Moderate–High

5. Cocker Spaniel

Cheerful and affectionate. Needs regular exercise and grooming but is a devoted family member.

Size: Medium · Exercise: High · Grooming: Moderate–High

6. Staffordshire Bull Terrier

Often called the "nanny dog" in the UK. Loyal, muscular and typically very fond of children when well raised.

Size: Medium · Exercise: High · Grooming: Low

7. Beagle

Merry, sturdy and good with kids. Can be vocal and needs enough exercise and training.

Size: Small–Medium · Exercise: High · Grooming: Low

8. Boxer

Playful and energetic. Good with children when socialised; needs space and daily exercise.

Size: Large · Exercise: Very High · Grooming: Low

9. Bernese Mountain Dog

Calm, gentle giants. Suit families with space and time for moderate exercise and grooming.

Size: Giant · Exercise: Moderate · Grooming: High

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best family dog in the UK?

Labrador and Golden Retrievers are perennially popular for good reason: they are friendly, trainable and typically excellent with children. Cavalier King Charles Spaniels and Cockapoos are great choices for smaller homes.

Are Staffies good family dogs?

Staffordshire Bull Terriers have a long history as family dogs in the UK and are often very good with children when well socialised and trained. They need daily exercise and consistent handling.
