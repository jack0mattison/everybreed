Frequently Asked Questions

Which is healthier, French Bulldog or Pug?

Both are brachycephalic and can have breathing and overheating issues. Pugs often live slightly longer (12–15 years) but both need careful breeding and vet care.

Which is more expensive in the UK, French Bulldog or Pug?

French Bulldogs are typically much more expensive (£2,000–£4,000+) due to demand and breeding challenges. Pugs are usually £1,000–£2,200.

Are French Bulldogs and Pugs good in flats?

Yes. Both are small, relatively low-energy and suit flat living if they get short walks and aren't overexerted in hot weather.
