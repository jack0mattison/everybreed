Best Dogs for Children (2026)

The best dogs for kids in the UK are typically patient, sturdy enough to handle gentle rough-and-tumble and predictable in temperament. Size isn't everything—some small breeds are great with children; some large breeds are gentle giants. Supervision and teaching children how to behave around dogs are always important.

The breeds below are often recommended for families with children. Always source from responsible breeders and socialise puppies early with kids and other animals.

1. Labrador Retriever

Friendly, tolerant and usually very good with children. Can be boisterous when young—supervision advised.

Size: Medium–Large · Exercise: High · Grooming: Moderate

2. Golden Retriever

Gentle and patient. A classic choice for families with space and time for exercise.

Size: Large · Exercise: High · Grooming: Moderate

3. Cavalier King Charles Spaniel

Small but sturdy and affectionate. Good with considerate children.

Size: Small · Exercise: Moderate · Grooming: Moderate

4. Staffordshire Bull Terrier

Often very fond of children when well raised. Strong and loyal; needs training and exercise.

Size: Medium · Exercise: High · Grooming: Low

5. Beagle

Merry and sturdy. Good with kids who understand boundaries; can be vocal.

Size: Small–Medium · Exercise: High · Grooming: Low

6. Cocker Spaniel

Cheerful and affectionate. Suits active families who can provide exercise and grooming.

Size: Medium · Exercise: High · Grooming: Moderate–High

7. Cockapoo

Friendly and adaptable. A good size for many families with children.

Size: Small–Medium · Exercise: Moderate · Grooming: Moderate–High

8. Bernese Mountain Dog

Calm and gentle with children. Needs space and moderate exercise.

Size: Giant · Exercise: Moderate · Grooming: High

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the safest dog for kids?

No dog is 100% safe without supervision and training. Labradors, Golden Retrievers and Cavalier King Charles Spaniels are often cited as among the best with children when socialised and supervised.
