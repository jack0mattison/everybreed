Best Dogs for Runners & Active Owners (2026)

If you run regularly in the UK, a breed that can keep up safely is a great companion. The best running dogs are typically medium to large, athletic and able to handle distance without joint or breathing issues. Avoid very young puppies and brachycephalic breeds for running.

Build up distance gradually and consider terrain and weather. The breeds below are often suited to active owners who run or hike regularly.

1. Labrador Retriever

Athletic and eager. Great running partner once fully grown; build up gradually.

Size: Medium–Large · Exercise: High · Grooming: Moderate

2. Golden Retriever

Stamina and willingness to be active. Suits long runs when fit.

Size: Large · Exercise: High · Grooming: Moderate

3. German Shepherd

High energy and endurance. Excellent for runners who can provide discipline and training.

Size: Large · Exercise: Very High · Grooming: Moderate

4. Border Collie

Incredible stamina and drive. Needs mental and physical challenge—running is a start.

Size: Medium · Exercise: Very High · Grooming: Moderate

5. Whippet

Fast and efficient. Prefers short, sharp bursts but can do longer runs when conditioned.

Size: Medium · Exercise: Moderate–High · Grooming: Low

6. Hungarian Vizsla

Built for endurance. Ideal for serious runners and active households.

Size: Medium–Large · Exercise: Very High · Grooming: Low

7. English Springer Spaniel

Energetic gundog. Great for trail runs and varied terrain.

Size: Medium · Exercise: Very High · Grooming: Moderate

8. Siberian Husky

Bred to run. Suits cold-weather runs; can overheat in summer.

Size: Medium–Large · Exercise: Very High · Grooming: Moderate

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best dog for running with?

Labradors, Golden Retrievers, German Shepherds, Border Collies and Vizslas are often recommended. Avoid brachycephalic breeds and build up distance slowly with any dog.
