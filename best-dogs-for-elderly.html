Best Dogs for Older People (2026)

Older owners in the UK often want a dog that is calm, manageable in size and doesn't require excessive exercise or grooming. The best dogs for elderly owners are typically gentle, loyal and happy with moderate daily walks and companionship.

Consider your mobility, ability to bend for grooming and pick up, and whether you can meet the dog's needs for the rest of its life. The breeds below are often recommended for older people, but individual dogs vary—meet the dog and breeder before committing.

1. Cavalier King Charles Spaniel

Affectionate, small and generally undemanding. Moderate exercise and grooming.

Size: Small · Exercise: Moderate · Grooming: Moderate

2. Cockapoo

Friendly and adaptable. Can be a good size for older owners who can manage moderate grooming.

Size: Small–Medium · Exercise: Moderate · Grooming: Moderate–High

3. Shih Tzu

Companionable lap dog. Needs coat care but is typically calm and devoted.

Size: Small · Exercise: Low–Moderate · Grooming: High

4. Pug

Low exercise needs and loving. Be mindful of breathing and heat; short walks suffice.

Size: Small · Exercise: Low–Moderate · Grooming: Low

5. Whippet

Calm indoors and easy to groom. Needs a safe run but not constant activity.

Size: Medium · Exercise: Moderate–High · Grooming: Low

6. Miniature Schnauzer

Loyal and trainable. Requires professional grooming but is a sturdy small dog.

Size: Small · Exercise: Moderate · Grooming: High

7. Cavapoo

Cavapoos are often gentle and small. Good for companionship with moderate care.

Size: Small–Medium · Exercise: Moderate · Grooming: Moderate–High

8. Pomeranian

Small and fluffy. Needs brushing but can be a lively little companion for active retirees.

Size: Small · Exercise: Low–Moderate · Grooming: High

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best dog for a 70 year old?

Cavalier King Charles Spaniels, Cockapoos, Shih Tzus and Whippets are often recommended: manageable size, moderate exercise and generally calm temperaments. Choose based on your mobility and grooming ability.
